Frequently Asked Questions

Can i choose the dentist on my own for treatment ?

+
Yes, you can choose your preferred dentist from our network of qualified professionals. We have experienced dentists available across all our clinics to provide you with the best care.

What are your dental clinic timings?

+
Our dental clinics are open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. We also offer emergency services for urgent dental care needs.

Will i get the same doctor each time i visit the same clinic ?

+
Yes, we try to ensure continuity of care by assigning you the same dentist for your ongoing treatments. However, in case of unavailability, another qualified dentist will take care of your needs.

How can I book an appointment?

+
You can book an appointment by calling our helpline, using our mobile app, visiting our website, or by directly visiting any of our clinic locations.

How you are able to provide less prices than other dental brands ?

+
We maintain competitive pricing through our efficient operational model, bulk procurement of materials, and our commitment to making quality dental care accessible to everyone.

How frequently should I visit a dentist?

+
We recommend visiting a dentist every 6 months for routine check-ups and cleanings. However, the frequency may vary based on your individual oral health needs.
